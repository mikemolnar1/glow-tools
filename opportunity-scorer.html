How the Score Works

Each opportunity is scored across 7 dimensions, each weighted by how much it actually predicts success. Answers are scored 1–10, multiplied by their weight, and summed to a final score out of 100.

The 7 Dimensions
Relationship Origin
20%
How the lead came in. Warm relationships close faster and at higher rates — this carries the most weight.
Decision Maker Access
15%
Can we get to the actual buyer? Procurement walls kill deals that should close.
Budget Potential
15%
How big can this get? Unknown budget scores neutral (5/10) — not penalized for early-stage opps.
Win Probability
15%
Fewer agencies = better odds. Unknown field scores neutral.
Expansion Potential
15%
Is this a one-off project or a gateway to something bigger? AOR potential scores highest.
Capability Fit
10%
How well does the work match what GLOW does best — category experience and work type combined.
Cost to Pursue
10%
Lower pitch cost = higher score. Full spec creative with major resource pull scores lowest.

The Door-Opener Flag

Some opportunities score low but are tactically brilliant — taking a small, cheap gig to get in front of a decision maker you otherwise couldn't reach. The Door-Opener toggle flags this intentionally, so the team knows it's a strategic move, not a mistake.
